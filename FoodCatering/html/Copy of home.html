Abc kitchen is a home based catering service. You get fresh food that is cooked to order. In our kitchen we only use stainless steel cookware. All the spice powders are made out from the scratch using whole spices.

The listed menu is our typical south Indian meal thats had on a daily/weekly basis. I also have listed few north Indian curries.

You can request any item thats not on the menu, we would be glad to oblige. We also do birthday parties, gettogether catering. Please place your order by 3pm.
